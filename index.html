SQL-Based Clustering & Equity Analysis

Dissertation by Camaren ¬∑ 2025

Abstract

This dissertation explores how SQL-driven clustering and principal component analysis (PCA) can reveal regional equity patterns in policy contexts. By leveraging scalable database logic and Power BI visualizations, the study aims to make complex technical insights accessible to decision-makers and the public.

Tools & Techniques

  • SQL for data modeling and clustering
  • Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
  • Power BI dashboards
  • DAX for policy-relevant metrics
